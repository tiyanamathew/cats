I LOVE CATS

Meet Nala. She is one of my two cats. We call her Nan for
short. She is a green-eyed tabby & she is very friendly.
Whenever I am feeling sad, I cuddle my cat & it always
makes me feel better. We rescued her from a neglected home &
she's now living a happy life. Cats, just like dogs, can help
uplift moods, & I really hope you consider getting a furry
friend of your own.
